# SOP: LevelupMax Operator Certification

**Version:** v1.0.0 | **Date:** 2026-03-01 | **SOP ID:** SOP-LUM-001

## Summary

This Standard Operating Procedure defines the LevelupMax operator certification program. Certification is mandatory for any human who interacts with an AINE beyond basic read-only access. The program ensures operators have the knowledge and skills to safely manage AI-Native Enterprises.

## Certification Levels

| Level | Title | Duration | Price | Access Granted |
|-------|-------|----------|-------|----------------|
| Level 1 | AINE Observer | 2 days | $800 | Read-only monitoring, incident recognition |
| Level 2 | AINE Operator | 3 days | $1,000 | Agent management, action approval, authority basics |

## Prerequisites

| Level | Prerequisite | Description |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Level 1 | None | Open to all professionals |
| Level 2 | Level 1 Certificate | Must hold valid Level 1 certification |

## Certification Governance

### Issuance

1. Candidate completes all required days of training
2. Candidate passes the assessment exam (80% threshold, minimum 70% per component)
3. LevelupMax issues a digitally signed certification token
4. Token is registered in the AINE's operator registry
5. ACTS logs the certification event

### Renewal

| Level | Renewal Period | Renewal Requirement | Renewal Duration |
|-------|---------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Level 1 | Annual (12 months) | 4-hour refresher + exam | Half day |
| Level 2 | Annual (12 months) | 6-hour refresher + exam | Full day |

### Revocation

Certification may be revoked if:

- Operator causes an incident through negligence
- Operator violates AINEFF governance policies
- Operator fails renewal exam
- NLO requests revocation with documented cause

Revocation is logged to ACTS and immediately removes all operator access.
